Validation and Verification

Validating and Verifying Bodies (VVOs) that wish to be part of our registration and certification program may apply for approval as assessors. This process must be initiated through a written communication, which must follow the guidelines established in the COLCX Procedure of the mitigation initiative cycle. It is essential that the request includes all the required documentation as stipulated in the procedure. This documentation will allow for a detailed assessment of the capability and competence of the LVO to ensure that they meet the standards of quality and rigor necessary for the validation and verification of GHG mitigation initiatives.

Process of approval of validation and verification organizations (ovv)

1

Submitting an Application to the COLCX Program

Responsible: OVV
Send to the e-mail gerenciatecnica.colcx@canalclima.com the written communication with the application, attaching the following documents
  • A. Certificate of Existence and Legal Representation or the document that takes its place.
  • B. Identity document of the legal representative.
  • C. Supporting documents of the accreditation under ISO 14065:2020 and ISO/IEC 17029:20191.
  • D. List of projects validated and/or verified with other programs or carbon standards, including among others the audits performed successfully and those that were rejected, the average time of duration of the audits and other management indicators that the OVV considers.
  • E. Duly signed Validation and Verification Agreement Form.
  • F. Any other documents deemed pertinent.
2

Application Review

Responsible: COLCX
The application and documents are thoroughly reviewed to confirm that they comply with the COLCX Standard.
3

Request for additional documents

Responsible: COLCX - OVV
Upon review of the application, the COLCX program may request additional documents for approval.
4

OVV approval

Responsible: COLCX
  • Once all the documents are available, the approval of the OVV before the COLCX program will be confirmed by e-mail.
  • In case the OVV is not approved, the decision will be communicated indicating the reason that gave rise to it.
5

Publication of the approved OVV in COLCX

It will be published within the program page as an approved OVV, specifying the Agency's scope and compliance.
