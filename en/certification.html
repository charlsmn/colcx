Certification
Program

We are a registration and certification program for multi-sector initiatives that, through programs or projects, reduce and remove GHG emissions from the atmosphere. We provide general guidelines on applicable standards and requirements for the certification of these initiatives and the registration of their mitigation results, ensuring that they comply with GHG mitigation fundamentals. We promote transparency, best practices and multi-stakeholder participation, relying on blockchain technology to ensure trust and security, enabling the tracking, transfer and communication of mitigation achievements.
