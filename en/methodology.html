Methodology

This section will manage the sectors and methodologies per sector of the COLCX program. Here you will be able to find detailed and updated information on the different areas of expertise and the specific methodologies applied to each sector, ensuring that all participants have access to the most relevant and effective approaches and practices.

COLCX Methodologies

Methodology for Greenhouse Gas Removals in Forest Plantations, Agroforestry Systems and Agricultural Activities

Guidance for the development of climate change mitigation projects related to forest plantations, agroforestry systems and agricultural activities, applicable to landscape management tools that are not located in forest areas, such as live fences, agroforestry systems, scattered trees in pastures, biodiversity cores, ecological corridors, among others.
