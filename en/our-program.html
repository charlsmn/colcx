Our Program

¿What is COLCX?

COLCX is a certification program created by Canal Clima SAS that seeks to contribute to mitigation goals through a standard that certifies greenhouse gas (GHG) mitigation initiatives that reduce and eliminate atmospheric emissions. We provide guidelines for certification and monitoring of results, promoting transparent and participatory practices. We use blockchain technology to ensure trust and security, allowing participants to track, transfer and share mitigation achievements.

Program objectives

To energize environmental markets and low-carbon development by contributing to sustainable development objectives, socio-environmental safeguards and integrity in carbon projects.

We help stakeholders mobilize climate action by achieving carbon neutrality, their mitigation targets, contribute to countries' NDCs and promote climate finance.

Important Landmarks COLCX

2018 Monitoring, reporting and verification of mitigation actions in Colombia is regulated - resolution 1447.
